#1. Hazi Nowab Ali High School - SSC Batch 2019

I spent the best moments of my life as a child. School life is surrounded by thousands of memories and thousands of stories. I get emotional when I look back at the memorable days of my school life. I want to go back to that beautiful time, the time when there was no need or getting. Now if someone asks me what is the sweetest memory of the past? Closing my eyes I will say, “School life”. What could be a more beautiful moment? I would say, never! Time has gone far! But I think I was younger a few days ago. I used to walk around all day. The brothers used to fight with a toy. My brother was two years younger than me. I was admitted to a school with two brothers. It was seen that every day I used to dirty my school dress and returned home, and my mother scolded us. In the end, mom made a trick to scare us. Still, who listens to whom, the two brothers used to return home with one or the other problem. When I got some time in school, I used to start jogging with my friends. Once I fell under a rickshaw while running. Still my mischief did not abate even a iota.
